Why Do You Need a Form Backend?

Static websites are built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They don’t run a server‑side language like PHP, Node.js, or Python. That means when someone fills out a form, there’s no backend to save the data, send emails, or trigger integrations.

FabForm solves this problem. Just point your form’s action to our endpoint, and we’ll handle submissions for you — storing data, sending notifications, and giving you a dashboard full of insights.

1. Add Your Form

Use a normal HTML form and set its action to FabForm.

2. Submit Data

When users hit “Submit,” FabForm securely collects and processes the data.

3. Get Results

You receive notifications, can export entries, and view everything in your dashboard.
